A Documentation Microsite

Dispatch

It is a documentation microsite template for your digital projects at IDEO

"The Deliverable is the Design"

— Someone said

Documentation sites are good for 3 reasons:

  1. Enabling a collaborative teamwork
    It is a collaborative environment for your design team to work together towards a project hand-off.

  2. Setting clients up for success
    It is a platform to present to your clients the depth of a Digital Design project. And to help your clients socializing the content of your project internally.

  3. Shipping and curating a living document
    It is a sharing space for development partners, and a reference / living document that evolves over time during a Stewardship phase.
